1. Identify your goals

    In all marketing plans it is important to set the objectives that will guide your strategy and social media marketing is no exception. Ideally, you should use SMART goals, which in other words means that your goals should be specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and temporary.


    INSTAGRAM

2.Create quality content

    The first step to creating quality content is to identify what type of information will be useful to your audience. Remember that your content must be entertaining
    and visually pleasing, as users will look at all these details to evaluate it.
    Also, do not forget that users do not like to be saturated with the same information, much less if it is only information from your company. Give them something that
    interests them and above all that is useful.
    On the other hand, you should take into account that, just as creating quality content takes time and effort, the ideal thing is to share it in the most possible channels
    so that it reaches as many potential customers as possible.

Use hashtags

    It is important that you use hashtags so that more people can find you, but you must be aware
    of which are the social networks in which hashtags are useful and which are not.
    For example, on Facebook they are not useful because nobody searches for hashtags on this social network,
    while on Instagram you have the option to search by hashtag and all the relevant content regarding that word or phrase appears.
